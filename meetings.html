MAT122

Analytics and Calculus I

A study of limits, derivatives, applications of derivatives, integrals, and applications of integrals. These topics are studied for a variety of functions of a single variable such as polynomial, rational, root, trigonometric, inverse trigonometric, and more.

MAT223

Analytics and Calculus II

A continuation of MAT 122. Applications of the definite integral, techniques of integration, parametric equations, polar coordinates, and infinite sequences and series are the main topics of this course.

Sp
MAT224

Analytics and Calculus III

A continuation of MAT 223. Vectors, vector functions, partial derivatives, multiple integrals, and vector calculus are the main topics of this course.
